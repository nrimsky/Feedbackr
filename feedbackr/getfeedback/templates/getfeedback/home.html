Feedbackr


Feedbackr is a super simple piece of software to help collect yes/no feedback on language model outputs from humans.

Just upload your prompt/completion dataset as a csv file and anyone will be able to score the completions via a generated link.

You can download the collected data from the platform at any point.

Please do not upload sensitive data, and remember that Feedbackr is still in development so may be completely reliable.
